Our Story

👋 Hi, my name is Carrie Wardencki-Currier (Owner), and I established Maid For You Housekeeping in 2015 with a mission to create a cleaner, healthier, and happier environment for all of my residential and business clients. Since I was a little girl, I’ve had a passion for cleaning and making spaces tidy. My hobby and passion quickly turned into a business as the increasing need for busy individuals, families, and companies to outsource these services was rapidly growing.

The business quickly grew by word of mouth, and today the company has a full staff of fully vetted, trustworthy, and meticulous individuals ready to tackle any size project. At Maid For You Housekeeping, we understand the value of a clean space. We believe that a clean environment is crucial for productivity, creativity, and overall well-being.

That’s why we’re passionate about providing top-quality home and business cleaning services that exceed your expectations. With over 10 years of experience in the cleaning industry, we have the expertise and qualifications to make that mission a reality. Our team of highly trained, qualified professionals is dedicated to providing exceptional cleaning services that leave your space spotless and sanitized. We are fully bonded, licensed, and insured, so you can have peace of mind while our professional staff does the dirty work!
